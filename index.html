About Me

I graduated from the University of Texas with a B.A. in Psychology with a 4.0 GPA and University Honors. I've been working at Bazaarvoice, a SaaS company based in Austin for 3 years. I'm a Systems Administrator in the IT department focused on Salesforce and NetSuite object based system maintenance and development.

I am the lead singer of the Bazaarvoice band, and every year we play in front of 1,000 people at our All Hands company anniversary party. This year we performed "Ex's and Oh's", "Cake By the Ocean", "Valerie", "Forget You", and "Bang Bang".
